Essential Castles — 10 Formations

Static rook and ranging rook castles explained in 3 lessons

A castle is a defensive formation that keeps your King safe. Which castle to use depends heavily on whether you play static rook (ibisha) or ranging rook (furibisha) — the formations are largely separate between the two strategies.

Here we cover 10 essential castles rated on three axes: moves to build, durability, and positional balance. Use these ratings to find the castle that fits your style.
